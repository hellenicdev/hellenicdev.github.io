This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit hellenicdev.github.io/hellenicdev (“we,” “us,” or “our”). By using the site, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information (Optional)

Email address — collected only if you voluntarily enter it into our form or sign up for the newsletter.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

Cookies — used for site functionality and analytics.

Google Analytics data — may include device information, browser type, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent on pages, and general usage patterns. Google Analytics does not provide us with personally identifiable information unless you have explicitly consented to such tracking through Google.

1.3 AI Chatbot Interactions

We provide an AI chatbot powered by SupportFast to assist users on our site. Interactions with the chatbot may be collected and stored to improve responses and website functionality. Chatbot conversations are processed by SupportFast’s systems but are not shared with other third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not require account creation and do not collect names, passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal data.

We collect information through:

We use the information we collect to:

Used to track website performance and usage patterns. Google may use aggregated data to improve its own products and services. Google’s privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

4.2 SupportFast (AI Chatbot)

Data from chatbot interactions may be shared with SupportFast for processing and improving AI responses. SupportFast’s use of your data is subject to their privacy policy.

No other third parties receive your information.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

We allow users under the age of 18, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children except when they voluntarily provide an email for the newsletter or interact with the chatbot.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has submitted personal data, they may contact us for removal.

If you sign up for the newsletter, we may use your email to send updates or announcements. You can opt out at any time by following the unsubscribe link included in every email or contacting us directly.

8. AI Chatbot Disclaimer

Please do not share sensitive personal information, passwords, or payment details with the AI chatbot. While interactions help improve responses, the chatbot is not monitored in real time and cannot guarantee confidentiality.
